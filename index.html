Biography

I am Harry Huang (aka Wenyuan Huang, 黄问远), a junior standing student of Math & CS at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. My primary areas of study include (Deep) Reinforcement Learning, Algorithm Optimization, and Computational Complexity. I have been reading literature on semi-supervised learning and reinforcement learning recently. At the same time, I am also deeply interested in Probability Theory and Game Theory.
I am an active competitive programming pariticipant, having won national first prizes every year during my three years of high school. On November 9, 2024, I won the champion of ICPC NCNA 2024.
Outside of academics, I excel in swimming, badminton and instrument playing. I started learning piano at the age of four and began teaching myself the cello after entering university, participating in a symphony/string orchestra for two and a half consecutive years.
